/ About Signsbeat

Signsbeat is a health tech startup based in Malaysia that focuses on turning biometric data into actionable health insights.

Riding the trend of wearable health tech - like smart rings and watches - the platform helps users monitor their lifestyle by tracking key behaviors like food intake, drinks, activity and supplement usage.

Through these insights, Signsbeat aims to empower people to make better, personalized health decisions in their daily lives.
